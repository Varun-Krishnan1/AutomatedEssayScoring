"Online friends can be global\nBeing able to connect with people from all over the world is one of the main reasons why online friendship are becoming more popular. And, with video calling and messaging apps, it's easy to keep in touch with your online friends, no matter how far away they are.Be cautious. You should always use caution  especially if your online friends have little or no connection to you in the real world. The more real-world connections you have with an online friend, the more confident you can be in sharing information with that person.With an online friend the things that you can share are limited, it usually goes from a link to a photo, while with an offline friend you can share everything: links, experiences, and memories"
